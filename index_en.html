About our school

This educational institution, organized in accordance with the Presidential decree of September 14, 2017, specializes in advanced study of physics, mathematics, computer science and astronomy.The school has an office of aerospace technologies, which has all the conditions for innovative education of children, including educational equipments VR BOX, HOLOMAX, QBOX. A telescope is installed on the roof of the building, through which students can observe celestial bodies. Open additional courses of robotics, young physicists, mathematicians, astronomers and computer scientists.

Opening ceremony

On September 4, the official opening ceremony of the specialized boarding school of astronomy, physics, mathematics and computer science named after Mirzo Ulugbek was held. Also, after the opening ceremony, the guests-participants of the ceremony organized lessons dedicated to the"day of knowledge". They were attended by: Minister of Innovative development-I. Abdurakhmonov; Director of AI an RUz-sh. a. Egamberdiev ; Deputy Minister of Innovative development-A. S. Nazarov.
